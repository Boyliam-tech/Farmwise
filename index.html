The Challenges in Modern Livestock Farming

Nigerian farmers face significant obstacles that impact productivity, profitability, and animal welfare

High Livestock Mortality

Undetected diseases lead to high mortality rates, causing significant financial losses for farmers who often discover issues too late for effective treatment.

Expensive Feed Options

Traditional feed sources are increasingly expensive and unsustainable, consuming up to 70% of operational costs for livestock farmers.

Inadequate Monitoring

Manual observation methods are inefficient for large herds, leading to missed health issues, breeding opportunities, and behavioral changes.

Waste Management Issues

Agricultural waste is poorly managed, creating environmental problems while valuable resources are discarded rather than utilized.
